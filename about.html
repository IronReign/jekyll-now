--- layout: page title: About Us --- Iron Reign - About Us
Iron Reign has been a robotics team for about 6 years now. We have won awards in multiple competitions, spanning from FLL (First Lego League), FTC (First Tech Challenge), to The Google Lunar X Prize challenge. The team today comprises of mostly Juniors and Sophomores and we hope to reach our very high goals not only in competition but also in our real world lives.
