Task: Finalize ball intake system

The ball intake mechanism of the robot has finally been given a mostly working design. The plastic ramp and the dual-row system of twist ties has been pretty effective with moderate power driving both rows. Reoccurring tests were created to observe what would function best; higher power in the first +lower in the second, lower in first+ higher in the second, or equal power for both. So far it is hard to tell which combination is better. Maybe that is because we were feeding the balls into the system by hand and driving the dual beaters with drills. We can test better when we have the motor mounted and the robot driving around. Also, right now the ramp is just duct-taped to the motors.
